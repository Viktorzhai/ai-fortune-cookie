# Launch Notes

## AI Fortune Cookie is live

Some tools generate code. Some tools review code. This one hands your repo a tiny silk robe, listens to the branch drama, and replies with a one-line fortune.

AI Fortune Cookie is a cross-agent skill for `Codex`, `Claude Code`, `Gemini CLI`, and `Cursor`. It keeps the elegant grammar of fortune-cookie wisdom, borrows only a whisper of project context, and delivers the result with clean, theatrical humor.

The goal was simple: make waiting less dull without turning the output into generic motivational sludge or overfit repo commentary.

So the skill tries to do three things at once:

- sound like an actual fortune cookie, not a status message
- notice the mood of the project without narrating the whole diff
- land with enough sparkle to make the terminal feel slightly more alive

## Sample fortunes

> This branch may be wearing clown shoes, but patience can still teach it enough manners to enter the palace.

> A noisy test suite is not your enemy; it is a dramatic little trumpet for the truth.

> Courage, dear builder: even tangled code can be persuaded to sit up straight.

## Why this is fun

- It treats one sentence like a constrained writing problem.
- It balances syntax, rhythm, humor, and project nuance instead of just dumping context.
- It works across the major coding-agent setups instead of living in one ecosystem.

## Tiny release pitch

If your build is still thinking, your tests are still arguing, or your refactor has entered its cape-swirling phase, this skill is here to keep morale absurdly overdressed.
